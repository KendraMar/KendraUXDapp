# UX Research

## Target Users

### Primary: Designers
- Working with prototypes and design artifacts
- Need context about design decisions and history
- Want to share and discuss designs with team members

### Secondary: Developers
- Consuming design artifacts and specifications
- Need to understand design rationale when implementing
- Want code-to-design traceability

### Tertiary: Product Managers
- Tracking design progress and decisions
- Need visibility into open design questions
- Want to see activity across integrated tools

## Jobs to Be Done

1. **Understand design context** — When working on a feature, quickly find relevant design decisions, open questions, and stakeholder context
2. **Track design progress** — See what's changed across tools without checking each one individually
3. **Collaborate on designs** — Discuss prototypes, share feedback, and resolve design questions
4. **AI-augmented workflows** — Use local AI to summarize context, suggest improvements, and automate routine design tasks

## Research Notes

Research findings should be documented here as studies are conducted. Link to external research repositories or tools when available.
